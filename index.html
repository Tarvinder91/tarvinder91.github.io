Hi, I’m Tarvinder Singh — a Cloud Architect and DevOps Leader with over 11 years of experience delivering scalable, automated cloud solutions across AWS, Azure and hybrid environments.

Currently, I’m leading cloud infrastructure strategy at Cognizant Technology Solutions Canada for one of our major client, helping them modernize their development platforms and maturing CI/CD processes.

I’m passionate about solving complex problems with elegant cloud-native architectures. I’ve been fortunate to work on and help establish several mainstream technologies when they were young — from AWS Transit Gateways and Landing zone implementations to designing and implementing Azure Machine Learning Data Analytics platforms. Currently, I work as a DevOps consultant for a major client, helping them transform their CI/CD journey and build a centralized platform and process for it.

When I’m not building infrastructure, I’m exploring innovations at the intersection of AI, DevOps, and cloud transformation. Or I would be occupied with with gardening, kickboxing or traveling - which are always part of my world :)

Whether it’s tech, philosophy, or fitness- I’m deeply obsessed with the fundamentals of everything. I’m excited to discover what new challenges lie ahead in this fast changing world and contribute in my ways. So I’m always eager to listen, learn, and contribute. After all, the great minds on this world are known for what they did, so it’s inevitable that your work is always a masterpiece.
